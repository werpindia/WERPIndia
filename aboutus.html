ABOUT US

WERP-India is a student initiative for women empowerment in India. A research-based social enterprise for empowering all Indian Women through social research and volunteering. About 50% of the world population is women. Their contributions to wealth and asset creation are substantial and can be inferred as same as that of men, though there is no precise estimate of such macro variables on the basis of gender. Despite this, in almost all societies, there is gender bias against women. The gender biases against women start from the conception in the womb till lifetime. The war against women is largely influenced by policies and issues of justice as well as the social construct that influence the mindset of the public against women. However, WERP-India has been born as a developmental project on the multi-dimensional project approach to women empowerment in India through social research techniques and volunteering. The social research project is expected to facilitate the reconstruction of Indian society towards a favourable mindset and appreciation for women and girls. It is noteworthy to mention that Women are the core drivers of all sustainable development. When a woman is empowered, the multiplier effect ripples down the family, the community and the nation at large. Women, the nurture of nature and nations.

VISION AND MISSION

Our Mission

To achieve women empowerment by faithful dedication and commitment to our five approaches

Our Vision

To see all Indian Women truly empowered in all areas of life

OUR APPROACH

FIVE (5) APPROACHES OF WERP-India

  • Students Initiatives – Design by the Students for the students for the Women. We are STUDENTS!
  • Social Research – Our Core Value.
  • Practical Involvement – We get involved.
  • Women Facilitation for Empowerment – We work for women.
  • Men Facilitation for Sustainable Development (M4sD) – We work to Facilitate Men for Favorable Environment for Women
